Human Rights

Overview

Human rights are basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person in the world. They include:

  • Right to life
  • Right to liberty and security of person
  • Right to freedom from discrimination
  • Right to freedom of thought, conscience, religion, and expression
  • Right to education and work
  • Right to health and well-being

Issues

Human rights issues exist globally and include issues like:

  • Discrimination
  • Violence and abuse
  • Access to education and healthcare
  • Freedom of speech and expression
